Home150

Closing Block 15 with synthesis. Israeli cuisine is the only national cuisine in the world that\'s younger than the country itself. It\'s still being invented. The "Israeli plate" — hummus + salad + pita + olive oil — is barely 75 years old. The synthesis: 12 traditions colliding on one small geography, mediated by Mizrahi peasant food, French technique, and a vegetable obsession.

Cultural note — When Israelis travel abroad and miss home, they don\'t miss schnitzel. They miss the salad — the one their grandmother chops every morning, with cucumber, tomato, parsley, lemon, olive oil. That salad is the one true universal Israeli food. Every Mizrahi, Ashkenazi, Arab, Druze, and Russian-Israeli kitchen makes a version. That\'s the synthesis.
