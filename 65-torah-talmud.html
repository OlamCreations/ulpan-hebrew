Bereshit — The Opening of Genesis

The first verses of the Torah. Bereshit 1:1-5. Read every year on Shabbat Bereshit (after Sukkot).

Hillel's Golden Rule (Talmud, Shabbat 31a)

A non-Jew asked Hillel to teach him the whole Torah while standing on one foot. Hillel's answer became foundational ethics.

Pirkei Avot — Ethics of the Fathers (selected sayings)

A Mishnah tractate of ethical maxims. Studied between Pessach and Shavuot. These sayings are quoted everywhere in Israeli culture.

The Priestly Blessing (Numbers 6:24-26)

Said by parents over their children every Shabbat. Also pronounced by Kohanim in synagogue.
