Health Funds (Kupot Cholim)

4 main funds — כְּלָלִית (Clalit, biggest, ~50%) · מַכַּבִּי (Maccabi, ~25%) · מְאוּחֶדֶת (Meuhedet, ~15%) · לְאוּמִית (Leumit, ~10%). You choose yours within 6 months of arrival.
