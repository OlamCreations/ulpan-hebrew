Misrad HaPnim (Interior Ministry)

Lesson 42 · משרד הפנים
Home42

Misrad HaPnim handles ID cards, passports, civil status, residency. The toughest bureaucracy in Israel — long lines, strict appointment system. You'll go many times. Bring patience and chocolate.

Booking — Use myvisit.gov.il to book appointments. Without an appointment you may wait hours or be turned away.
