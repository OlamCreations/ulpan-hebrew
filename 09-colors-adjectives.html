Like verbs, adjectives and colors agree with the noun in gender and number. The adjective ALWAYS comes after the noun, and takes the article if the noun is definite.

Agreement rule — For "the big book" → ה (article) on both: הַסֵּפֶר הַגָּדוֹל (ha-sefer ha-gadol). For "a big book" → no article: סֵפֶר גָּדוֹל (sefer gadol).
