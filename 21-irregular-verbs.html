Irregular Verbs

Lesson 21 · פעלים חריגים
Home 20 21

The most-used verbs are often the most irregular. They contain "weak" letters (gutturals: א ה ח ע, or ו י in the root) that change conjugation. These are the daily verbs: go, come, give, take, put, get up, sit / live, live, build, die.

הָלַךְ — to go / walk

בָּא — to come / arrive

נָתַן — to give

לָקַח — to take

שָׂם — to put / place

קָם — to get up

יָשַׁב — to sit / live (reside)

חַי — to live (be alive)

בָּנָה — to build

מֵת — to die
