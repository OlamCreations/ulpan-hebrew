The Government & Cabinet

Lesson 123 · הַמֶּמְשָׁלָה
Home123

The PM, ~25 ministers, deputy ministers. The Cabinet (הַמֶּמְשָׁלָה) meets every Sunday morning at the PM\'s office. Ministers run ministries (מִשְׂרָדִים), control budgets, and answer to the Knesset. Knowing which minister handles what — Treasury, Defense, Interior, Education, Justice — lets you navigate any Israeli bureaucratic process.

Cultural note — Sunday morning Cabinet (יְשִׁיבַת הַמֶּמְשָׁלָה) is a media spectacle. PMs deliver "opening remarks" (דִּבְרֵי פְּתִיחָה) that drive the week\'s news cycle. The PM\'s residence on Balfour Street in Jerusalem became the focal point of the 2020-2021 protests. Kohelet Forum, Brothers in Arms, and other civil society orgs constantly engage with ministries.
