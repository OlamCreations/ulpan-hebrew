Lesson 98 · הומור ישראלי
Home98

Israeli humor is direct, self-deprecating, often dark, and built on the shared experience of bureaucracy, war, and mothers. Understanding the joke is understanding the country.

Cultural reality — Israelis joke about everything: army, politics, religion, death, mothers, ethnic differences (Ashkenazi vs Sephardic vs Mizrahi). Nothing is sacred. This isn't disrespect — it's the survival mechanism of a small country with too much history.
