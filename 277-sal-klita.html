Sal Klita — The Aliyah Package

Lesson 277 · סַל קְלִיטָה
Home277

Sal Klita ("Absorption Basket") is the Israeli government's financial support package for new olim. Total over 5-7 years: ~30,000-100,000 NIS depending on family size, age, country of origin, and other variables. Key components: kupat klita (monthly stipend, 6-12 months), housing rental subsidy (sechirah), Hebrew ulpan funding, retraining funds (chofesh limudim), tax breaks, kupa healthcare, mortgage discounts. Sal Klita is automatic upon Misrad HaKlita registration — but you must claim each component.

Cultural note — Sal Klita is the most underutilized resource for olim. Most know about kupat klita stipend; fewer know about retraining funds (chofesh limudim 50% subsidy for studies), discounted university tuition (~50% reduction first 3 years), free Hebrew up to Hey level, employment-focused training. Anglo olim often miss out by not knowing the system; Russian-Israeli vatikim guide newcomers. As of 2024, the digital portal (gov.il) automated some claims. Single olim get less than couples; couples less than families with kids; oleh hadash from "distress countries" (Ukraine, Venezuela) get expanded packages.
