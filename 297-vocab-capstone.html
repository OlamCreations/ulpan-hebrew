Vocabulary Capstone — 5,000 Words for B2/C1

Lesson 297 · סִכּוּם אוֹצַר מִילִים
Home297

CEFR B2 active vocabulary is roughly 4,000-5,000 words. Passive comprehension covers ~10,000. By this lesson you should know: ~1,000 high-frequency function words, ~1,500 daily-life nouns, ~1,000 verbs across binyanim, ~500 abstract concepts, ~500 domain-specific terms (politics, business, health, tech). This lesson catalogs the highest-impact word families to fill gaps. Use the Anki SRS technique for the last 1,000 words you don\'t know.

Cultural note — Hebrew word frequency follows Zipf — the top 100 words cover ~50% of any text. The next 1,000 cover ~75%. Beyond that, vocabulary growth is domain-specific. The fastest B2→C1 path: read 3 articles per day in 3 different domains (news, business, science), look up unknown words, add to Anki, review daily. 1,000 new words in 6 months is realistic.
