Israel was energy-poor for decades — coal and imported oil. The 2009 discovery of the Tamar gas field (off Haifa) and 2010 Leviathan changed everything. Israel now exports gas to Egypt and Jordan. Solar penetration is high (~17% of electricity). The Israel Electric Corporation (חַח"י) is the historic monopoly. Water — desalination, reuse — Israel pioneered the modern toolkit.

Cultural note — "מַתְמוֹן הַגַּז" (the gas treasure) is a politically charged phrase. Royalty rates for gas have been hotly debated since 2010. Sovereign wealth fund (קֶרֶן הַמַּטְמוֹן הַלְּאֻמִּי) — Israel\'s small fund holding gas revenues — was created in 2014 but disbursements are politically contentious. The Mediterranean is the energy frontier.
