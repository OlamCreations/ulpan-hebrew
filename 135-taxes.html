Taxes — Income, VAT, & The Rest

Lesson 135 · מִיסּוּי
Home135

Israel\'s tax system is progressive, complex, and pervasive. Income tax brackets up to 50%. VAT (מַע"מ) at 18% (raised from 17% in 2025). Bituach Leumi (national insurance) ~12% combined. Property tax (אַרְנוֹנָה) varies by city. Lesson 47 covered tax filing basics; this lesson covers the structural picture — what gets taxed and at what rate.

Cultural note — Most Israeli salaried workers do not file annual income tax returns — the employer withholds and reports. Self-employed (עוֹסֵק מֻרְשֶׁה) must file. The Israeli tax authority is "מַס הַכְנָסָה" (Mas Hachnasa) — feared and respected. Olim hadashim get major tax breaks for ~10 years. The "אֲהֲדָה" — sympathy/benefit — for olim is real and substantial.
