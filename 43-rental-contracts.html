Rental Contracts

Lesson 43 · חוזה שכירות
Home43

A rental contract in Israel is detailed and tightly worded. It includes guarantor (arev), bank guarantees (arvut bankait), security deposit (pikadon). Understanding the legal vocabulary protects you from disputes.

Standard rental year — Most leases are 1 year with 1-year renewal option. 2-month security deposit + bank guarantee + 1-2 guarantors are typical. Furniture (mefurash / lo mefurash) is a key clause.
