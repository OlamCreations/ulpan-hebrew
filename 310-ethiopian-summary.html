Block 31 closes the Ethiopian/Beta Israel section. The community: ~150K+ Israeli citizens, ancient pre-Talmudic Jewish heritage, dramatic 1980s-90s mass aliyah. Lessons covered: Beta Israel ancient history, Operation Moses 1984, Operation Solomon 1991, Falash Mura, Amharic linguistic influence, Ethiopian food culture, Sigd festival, notable figures, integration generations. After Block 31 you understand the Ethiopian-Israeli journey from Gondar mountains to Israeli mainstream — one of modern Jewish history\'s most dramatic chapters.

Cultural note — Ethiopian-Israelis represent the most dramatic cultural integration story in modern Israel: walking from Ethiopia, airlifts during civil war, building lives across language, religion, race differences. Knowing this lexicon = navigating Bat Yam, Petach Tikva, Beit Shemesh communities; understanding Sigd; reading Pnina Tamano-Shata\'s political moves; recognizing Hagai Tzippori on the IDF marathon team. After Block 31 you\'ve covered the unique Ethiopian dimension of the Israeli story.
