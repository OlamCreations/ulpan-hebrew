Useful Phrases on Arrival

Misrad HaKlita (משרד הקליטה) — Ministry of Aliyah & Integration. First stop at Ben Gurion airport: they issue your Teudat Olé and the first Sal Klita payment.
Teudat Olé / Teudat Zehut — Olé card (new immigrant) issued on arrival, valid ~6 months. Used to apply for the Teudat Zehut (Israeli ID) at Misrad HaPnim.
