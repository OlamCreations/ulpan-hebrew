צַהַ"ל (Tzahal) — Tzva HaHagana l\'Yisrael — Israel\'s Defense Forces. Established 1948. Three branches: ground (חֵיל יַבָּשָׁה), air (חֵיל אֲוִיר), navy (חֵיל הַיָּם). Plus intelligence, computing, medical, and special direction. ~170,000 active, ~465,000 reserves. Mandatory service: men 32 months, women 24 months. Lesson 71 covered the basics for olim; this block goes deep.

Cultural note — "צה"ל" is read aloud as "tzahal" (one word). Israelis under 50 served. The army is THE common shared experience that crosses all Israeli cleavages — religious, secular, Mizrahi, Ashkenazi, periphery, center. "אֵיפֹה שֵׁרַתָּ?" — "Where did you serve?" — is a mid-introduction question, never offensive.
