Israeli Water Diplomacy

Lesson 179 · דִּיפְּלוֹמַטְיַת הַמַּיִם
Home179

Israeli water is regional. The 1994 Israel-Jordan peace treaty includes water-sharing clauses (50M m³/year to Jordan). The Palestinian Water Authority manages West Bank/Gaza supply through joint Israeli-Palestinian committees. New "Prosperity" agreements with UAE/Bahrain/Morocco include desal cooperation. Cyprus and Greece import Israeli desal expertise. Israel-Egypt water relations are sensitive but stable. This lesson covers the geopolitical layer of water — useful for any career path engaging foreign relations.

Cultural note — In Israel, water is geopolitical glue and friction. The Jordan Valley\'s fate is tied to water rights. The Gaza desalination plant (Israel-built, EU-funded) is a humanitarian baseline. The Eastmed pipeline talks (gas-to-Cyprus-to-Italy) include water deals. Speaking about water in international Hebrew gives you immediate credibility — and you can listen to Israeli foreign ministry briefings without translation.
