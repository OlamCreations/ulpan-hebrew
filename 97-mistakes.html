Every olé makes the same 30 mistakes. Knowing them in advance saves you from a year of being misunderstood at the bakery. Some are grammatical, some are pronunciation, some are cultural — but all are fixable.

Cultural reality — Israelis will correct your Hebrew loudly and immediately. This isn't rude — it's their way of helping. Don't take it personally. The fastest way to improve is to make mistakes in public daily.
