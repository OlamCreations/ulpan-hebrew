The Defense Industry

Lesson 138 · תַּעֲשִׂיָּה בִּטְחוֹנִית
Home138

Israel is the world\'s 8-10th largest arms exporter — ~$13B/year in 2023, ~$15B+ in 2024. Three primary state-owned/state-influenced firms: IAI (Israel Aerospace Industries), Rafael (advanced weapons), Elbit (private, electronics). Defense overlaps tech: many startups commercialize ex-IDF tech. Drones, anti-missile systems (Iron Dome / Arrow / David\'s Sling), surveillance — defining Israeli specialties.

Cultural note — Israeli defense companies recruit heavily from IDF tech units. The defining career path: 8200 → master\'s degree → Rafael / Elbit / IAI / startup. Major defense exits: NSO Group (Pegasus), Cellebrite. The phrase "תַּעֲשִׂיָּה בִּטְחוֹנִית" is treated as one of the strategic pillars of the country alongside high-tech and academia.
