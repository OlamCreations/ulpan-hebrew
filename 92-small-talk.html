Small Talk & Conversations

Lesson 92 · שיחות יומיומיות
Home92

Israelis don't really do "polite small talk." They skip the weather and ask you why you're not married, how much you paid for your apartment, and what you think of the government — within 90 seconds of meeting you. Learn the real rhythm of Israeli conversation.

Cultural reality — In most cultures, personal questions come after trust. In Israel, personal questions ARE how trust is built. "How much do you earn?" isn't rude — it's interest. If someone doesn't ask you anything personal, that's the cold version.
