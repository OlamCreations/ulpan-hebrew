Israel has shuks (open-air markets) for produce, supermarkets, and modern malls. The shuk is theater — bargaining, friendly arguments, samples. Vocabulary for both worlds.

Shuk Machane Yehuda (שוק מחנה יהודה) Jerusalem · Shuk HaCarmel (שוק הכרמל) Tel Aviv · Shuk Talpiot Haifa. Best Friday before Shabbat: discounts, energy, food samples.
