IDF History — From Hagana to Tzahal

Lesson 157 · תּוֹלְדוֹת צה"ל
Home157

The IDF was founded May 26, 1948, weeks after Israeli independence. Predecessors: Hagana (mainstream defense), Etzel/Irgun, Lehi (smaller paramilitaries). Major wars: 1948 Independence, 1956 Sinai, 1967 Six-Day, 1973 Yom Kippur, 1982 Lebanon, 2006 2nd Lebanon, multiple Gaza operations. Each war re-shaped Israeli identity. Lesson 56 covered Israeli history broadly; this one is about the army specifically.

Cultural note — Israeli wars are remembered by year and casualty count: "67" — Six-Day, "73" — Yom Kippur, "82" — Lebanon, "23-24" — Iron Swords. Yom HaZikaron (Memorial Day) honors all fallen soldiers; one minute of silence brings the entire country to a standstill — cars stop on highways, buses pull over, every shop and café falls silent.
