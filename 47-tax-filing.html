Tax Filing (Mas Hachnasa)

Lesson 47 · מס הכנסה
Home47

Israeli tax system: progressive income tax (mas hachnasa), VAT (ma'am 17%), municipal property tax (arnona). New olim get a 10-year exemption on foreign income — huge benefit. Vocabulary for filing, deductions, refunds.

10-year olim exemption — Income earned outside Israel (US salary, foreign rental, foreign pension) is tax-exempt for 10 years from your aliyah date. Major retention tool — consult a CPA before any major financial move.
