Israeli weather is Mediterranean: hot dry summers (May-October), cool wet winters (November-April). The Negev is desert. Snow in Jerusalem maybe once a year. Vocabulary for daily small talk and weather forecasts.

Mezeg avir (מֶזֶג אֲוִויר) — literally "temperament of air" = weather. The forecast is "tachazit mezeg ha-avir".
