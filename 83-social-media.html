Israeli social media: WhatsApp groups for everything (family, building, kids' class, oleh communities). Facebook strong, Instagram for younger, X/Twitter for politics. TikTok everywhere. Vocabulary for posts, reactions, and online life.

WhatsApp etiquette in Israel — Voice messages (sherutei kol) are NORMAL — even from strangers. People send 3-minute audio messages instead of typing. Get used to it. Texting at midnight is fine. "Lo nora" (no big deal) is the response to most apologies.
