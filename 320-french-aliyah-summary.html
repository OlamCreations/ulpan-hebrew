Closing Block 32 — the French Aliyah arc. From Rashi (1040, Troyes) to the Drahi-funded i24NEWS, from the Dreyfus trial to Hyper Cacher, from ulpan classrooms in Netanya to mufleta tables in Ashdod. You now have the historical anchors, the geographic triangle, the institutional vocabulary, and the cultural code-switching to understand and inhabit French-Israeli identity in modern Hebrew. This lesson consolidates the fifty most-used terms from lessons 311-319.

Cultural note — French olim are roughly 4% of Israel\'s population but punch far above their weight in media (i24NEWS, Drahi), real estate (Netanya, Ashdod, Ra\'anana), gastronomy (boulangeries, kosher patisseries), and religious life (Sephardic-Ashkenazi mix). The 2012-2020 wave (~50K olim) was the largest from any Western country. Knowing this vocabulary is knowing a real piece of contemporary Israel.
