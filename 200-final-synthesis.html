Lesson 200 — the synthesis of your journey from aleph-bet to functional Hebrew fluency. Across 200 lessons you have covered foundations, grammar, daily life, and culture in lessons 1-100; the Hebrew Bible in 101-110; Talmud and halacha in 111-120; modern Israeli politics in 121-130; the economy in 131-140; cuisine in 141-150; the IDF in 151-160; Yiddish in 161-170; water, sanitation and agriculture in 171-180; Arabic-shared heritage in 181-190; advanced grammar, literature, media and conversation in 191-200. You now hold roughly 3000+ active vocabulary words, all 7 binyanim, and every register from biblical to slam-poetry. This is the end of the course and the beginning of real Hebrew.

Cultural note — Reaching lesson 200 means you have the level needed to start a real Ulpan in Israel and integrate from day one. CEFR-wise: solid B1 in receptive Hebrew, A2-B1 in active production. The next phase is ulpan plus immersion — you will plateau in 1-3 months, then accelerate again as living context fills the gaps a textbook cannot. B1 to B2 takes another 3-6 months. B2 to C1 takes another 1-2 years of regular use. You are past the hardest part — the runway is now downhill.
