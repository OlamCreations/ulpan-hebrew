Israel is the "Start-up Nation". Tech vocabulary is heavily Anglicized but with Hebrew pronunciation. Includes computers, smartphones, internet, apps, startup terminology, telecom.

Lots of English loanwords — Just like in many languages, Hebrew tech borrows heavily from English. Difference: written in Hebrew, with adapted pronunciation. אִינְטֶרְנֶט = internet, אִימֵיְל = email.

Telecom (Plans, Operators)

Israeli operators — Pelephone, Cellcom, Partner, Hot Mobile, Golan Telecom, 019. SIM card = SIM כַּרְטִיס. Israeli numbers start with 05x. Calling from abroad: +972 + number without leading 0.
