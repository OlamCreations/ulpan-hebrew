Phrases

Declaration of Independence — Opening (May 14, 1948)

Read aloud by David Ben Gurion at the Tel Aviv Museum on 5 Iyar 5708 (May 14, 1948), 4 PM. The most important political text in modern Hebrew. Recited every Yom HaAtzmaut.

Rabin's Last Speech — Peace Rally (Nov 4, 1995)

Yitzhak Rabin's final speech at the peace rally in Kings of Israel Square (now Rabin Square). Minutes later he was assassinated. The phrase "violence is undermining the foundations of Israeli democracy" became iconic.

Yizkor — Memorial Prayer for Fallen Soldiers

Recited on Yom HaZikaron at every memorial ceremony. The military version ("Yizkor for IDF Fallen") replaces the standard "May God remember" with "May the people of Israel remember."
