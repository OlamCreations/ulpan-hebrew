Asking and giving directions in Israel — including reading street signs, navigating public transport, dealing with Israeli bluntness when getting directions ("turn left, then right, then stop the next person and ask").

Cultural reality — Israelis love giving directions even if they don't actually know the way. They'll point confidently in any direction. Best strategy: ask 2-3 different people. Or use Waze (Israeli invention).
