The complete vocabulary of becoming an Israeli citizen — from the Sochnut (Jewish Agency) office in your home country to your first 90 days in Israel. Every olim needs these words.

Olim path — Open file with Sochnut → Shaliach interview → consul approval → Teudat Olé issued at Ben Gurion → Misrad HaKlita stipend → choose city/Ulpan → Misrad HaPnim for Teudat Zehut → Bituach Leumi → bank account → Klita continues for ~3 years.
